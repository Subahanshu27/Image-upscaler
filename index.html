Why Seed VR 2 Produces Better Results

Most upscalers sharpen what is already there. Seed VR 2 generates what should be there.

Diffusion-Based, Not Filter-Based

Older models like Real-ESRGAN apply learned sharpening filters. Seed VR 2 uses a diffusion architecture that generates new, plausible detail pixel by pixel. The difference is visible on complex textures: skin pores, individual hairs, woven fabric, tree bark.

Faces That Look Like Faces

Face upscaling is where most tools break. Seed VR 2 is trained on high-fidelity face data, so it recovers realistic eyes, skin texture, and facial structure instead of the waxy, over-smoothed look you get from generic upscalers.

Handles Every Image Type

Portraits, landscapes, product shots, screenshots, pixel art, old scanned photos. Seed VR 2 adapts to the content rather than applying one strategy to everything. Text stays sharp. Gradients stay smooth. Edges stay clean.

Open Source, Not a Black Box

Seed VR 2 is developed by ByteDance and available as an open-source model. You can inspect the architecture, read the paper, and verify the results. Most competitors hide their models behind "proprietary AI" with no way to evaluate what you are getting.

How Does AI Image Upscaling Work?

An AI image upscaler is a tool that uses a neural network to increase image resolution while adding realistic detail. Unlike simple interpolation (which blurs the image as it stretches), AI upscaling models are trained on millions of image pairs at different resolutions. The model learns what high-resolution detail should look like for a given low-resolution input. When you upload an image, the AI analyzes patterns, textures, and edges, then generates new pixels that match what the higher-resolution version would look like. The result is a larger, sharper image with recovered detail rather than blurry enlargement.

What Is Seed VR 2?

Seed VR 2 is an image super-resolution model developed by ByteDance. It uses a diffusion-based architecture to generate high-quality upscaled images, handling complex textures like skin, hair, fabric, and natural scenes with high fidelity. Unlike older upscaling models (Real-ESRGAN, BSRGAN), Seed VR 2 can generate plausible new detail rather than just sharpening existing pixels. Floyo runs Seed VR 2 as a managed ComfyUI workflow through its API, so you get state-of-the-art quality without setting up infrastructure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

What image formats do you support?

JPG, PNG, and WebP for upload. Download in any of those formats. We recommend PNG for maximum quality.

How much can I upscale an image?

Up to 8x the original resolution. A 1000x1000 image becomes 8000x8000. You can choose 2x, 4x, or 8x.

Will upscaling make a blurry photo sharp?

The AI can recover significant detail from blurry or low-resolution images, but results depend on the source quality. It works best on images that are low-resolution but otherwise clean. Heavily compressed or extremely blurry images will improve but will not look like they were shot at 8K.

Can I upscale images for print?

Yes. Upscaling to 4x or 8x typically gives you enough resolution for high-quality printing at 300 DPI. A 1000px image at 4x becomes 4000px, which prints at about 13 inches at 300 DPI.

What AI model powers this tool?

Seed VR 2 by ByteDance. It is a diffusion-based super-resolution model that generates realistic detail rather than just sharpening pixels. It runs as a ComfyUI workflow on Floyo's infrastructure.

How long does it take?

No. Images are processed on our servers and deleted after your session. We do not store, share, or use your images for training.
